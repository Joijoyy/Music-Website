The Lineup Artists-Headliners

Stonebwoy

Stonebwoy is the stage name of Livingstone Etse Santekla, a Ghanaian afropop, dancehall and reggae artiste. He is the CEO of Burniton Music Group. In 2015, he won Best International Act Africa at the 2015 BET Award and Artist of the year in 2015. He also won the Artist of the year at the Ghana Music Awards. Some of his hit songs are Bawasaaba, Mane Me, Tomorrow amongst others.

Becca

Rebecca Akosua Acgeampomaa Acheampong, known mononymously as Becca, is a Ghanaian singer, songwriter and actress. She was contestant on the second season of TV3's annual singing competition, Mentor. Her debut studio album Sugar, was released in 2007. It earned her five nominations at the 2008 Ghana Music Awards. Some of her songs Na Wash, Beshiwo, Hw3 really hitted big time.

Eazi

Oluwatosin Oluwole Ajibade, better known by his stage name Mr. Eazi, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and enterpreneur. He is the pioneer of Banku Music, a fusion sound he describes as a mixture of Ghanaian highlife and Nigerian chord progressions and patterns. Leg Over, Bad Vibes, Akwaaba are some of his popular songs.
