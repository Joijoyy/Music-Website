Breezy

A new breeze of air has entered the music industry. Barbara Naa Okailey Nyarko is her name and is also called ‘Breezy’. She is a multi-talented
young artiste born on 4th December 1998, and attended Ghana Senior
High School, Koforidua in the Eastern Region of Ghana.
Her presence in the music industry started off as a dancer although she was singing and wanted to play the guitar from childhood.

Breezy became known by schoolmates and the public as ‘skelebarbs’ because of her ability to dance. Naa won several certificates for being the best dancer in her former senior high school.
Upon completing her senior high school education, she wanted to
pursue her musical career.

She has been practicing the acoustic guitar for some time now and
did few song covers by popular artists like Eugene’s ‘Angela’, and
Seyi Shay’s ‘Touch Me’. Immediately after performing these songs,
she started being creative with her own songs.

Breezy recorded her first single with ‘Reynolds the Gentleman’,
a multi-instrumentalist and producer. He discovered her on Instagram with a song titled ‘Nea Woyeyi’ which featured Reynolds himself.
She performed at places like ‘Piano Bar’, ‘Serallio’, ‘Badu Lounge’, and ‘High School Events’ that invited Artistes from the mainstream like Gemini,
Edem, etc.

Breezy’s second song titled ‘Kambu’ produced by Standec which was
released on the 4th of December 2017, got her known in various tertiary
institutions in Ghana. She then released her third song which featured
popular Dancehall/Reggae artiste Expixode in a song titled: ‘Ma Mind dey’.

Recently, Naa made a soul version of Stonebwoy’s ‘Tomorrow’ and
'Gbedegbede’ that got Stonebwoy really interested in her style of music.
